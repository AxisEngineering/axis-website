Privacy Notice

This notice explains what personal data Axis Engineering collects through this website, why we collect it, and your rights over it under UK GDPR.

Who we are

This website is operated by Axis Engineering, Unit 15, Elms Business Park, Atherstone Road, Swadlincote DE12 7AP, United Kingdom. For any privacy question, contact sales@axisengineering.co.uk. Axis Engineering is the data controller for the information described here.

What we collect

We only collect what you choose to send us through the enquiry form. That is:

  • Your name
  • Your company (optional)
  • Your email address
  • The application type you select
  • The one-line duty / project details you write

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies, and we do not build marketing profiles from this data.

Why we collect it

We use these details for one purpose: to read, understand and respond to your enquiry, and to hold any follow-up conversation about the work. The lawful basis is our legitimate interest in responding to a business enquiry you have initiated.

How the form is processed

The enquiry form is submitted through Web3Forms, a form-delivery service that forwards your submission to our email inbox. Your details pass through Web3Forms only to deliver that message; see the Web3Forms privacy policy for how they handle data in transit. From there, your enquiry sits in our own email system.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry details for as long as needed to deal with your enquiry and any resulting project or relationship, after which they are deleted. If an enquiry does not lead to work, we remove it once it is clearly no longer relevant.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask us to:

  • Confirm what data we hold about you and give you a copy
  • Correct anything that is inaccurate
  • Delete your data where there is no ongoing need to keep it
  • Restrict or object to how we use it

To exercise any of these, email sales@axisengineering.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
